Privacy Policy

At TrendNest, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use, share, and protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information when you use our website:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, etc.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, etc.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including IP addresses, browser type, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To communicate with you, including sending order confirmations and promotional emails (with your consent).
  • To improve the functionality of our website.
  • To analyze and monitor usage patterns to enhance user experience.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following cases:

  • With service providers who help us operate our website and process transactions.
  • With law enforcement or other authorities if required by law.
  • In connection with business transfers, such as mergers or acquisitions.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-out of receiving promotional emails at any time.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us personalize content, analyze traffic, and remember your preferences. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
